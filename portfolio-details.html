Atliq Mart Revenue Insight in Hospitality Domain

Project information

  • Tool Used : Power BI
  • Project URL : Live Interactive Dashboard
About this Project :-

Atliq Grands noticed a loss in their market share and revenue over a few months. To understand the cause of this loss, they needed a way to analyze this. I created a dashboard in Power BI using three months of data. With this created dashboard, Revenue team of AtliQ Grands were able to gain insights about their revenue trend. This could help in regaining their revenue and market share by 20% in the next month.

Insights from the Dashboard :-
  1. Across all 4 city, Average rating ranged from 3.41 to 3.78, also week 3 in month June made up 6.73% of Average Rating.
  2. Mumbai had the highest Revenue at 660641658, followed by Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Delhi.
  3. ADR and Realisation % diverged the most when the booking_platform was direct offline, when ADR were 1279305.65% higher than Realisation %.
  4. Across all 7 booking_platform, Realisation % ranged from 69.80% to 70.57% and ADR ranged from 12,638.15 to 12,793.76.
  5. W 27 in Month July made up 7.71% of RevPAR.
  6. July had the highest average RevPAR at 7,450.53, followed by May at 7,340.54 and June at 7,247.47
  7. Occupancy % for Business (58.12%) was higher than Luxury (57.59%).
